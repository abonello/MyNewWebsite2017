About

As a Sound Artist I engage in many activities related to the artistic use of sound. These range from Composition and Sound Design, Performance and Conducting, to Music/Sound Production and Teaching. I am also active in research and product development.

Thank you for visiting my web site. Please feel free to roam this website and leave comments using the contact page.

Any commissions or project ideas, including collaborative artistic work are welcome.

Private Music Teaching Service

I teach students of various ages, ranging from 7 years to adults. I follow the ABRSM syllabus although exams are not compulsory.

I am based in Guildford and students come from various areas around Guildford as well as Woking, Normandy and Ash.

    Instruments / subjects
  • Piano
  • Brass instruments - Trumpet, Cornet, Flugelhorn, French Horn, Baritone Horn, Euphonium, Trombone, Tuba
  • Theory of Music
  • Composition
  • Music Technology - including MIDI, Sound Recording, Sound Design and Synthesis

I have several years of experience in teaching supported by academic and professional training.
